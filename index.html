Features

  • Chat: Interact with AI to generate policies, plans, and analysis
  • Policies: Create and manage compliance policies
  • Plans: Generate security and compliance plans
  • Frameworks: Browse compliance standards and requirements
  • Controls: Document and track organizational control implementations
  • Procedures: Manage operational procedures with steps and execution tracking
  • Analytics: Track your work and activity

Supported Frameworks

  • NIST CSF 2.0
  • NIST SP 800-53
  • NIST CMMC 2.0
  • HIPAA
  • HITRUST
  • SOX
  • SOC 2
  • GDPR
  • CCPA
  • CJIS
  • PCI DSS

  • Compare multiple frameworks for alignment
  • Track control implementations and procedures in the Controls tab
  • Review analytics to track compliance efforts
